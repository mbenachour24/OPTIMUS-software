Welcome to Optimus

How to Use This Platform

Optimus is an interactive system for analyzing the interaction between political and judicial systems. Use the navigation menu to explore different sections:

  • Political System: Create and manage legal norms.
  • Judicial System: Review cases and evaluate their constitutionality.
  • View Cases: Browse pending and resolved legal cases.
  • View Norms: Check existing norms and their status.
  • General Log: Track system activities and key events.
  • Statistics Dashboard: View real-time analytics on system performance.
